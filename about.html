About me

I am a third year student studying full time Creative Digital Media at Institute of Technology Blanchardstown. I am deaf but that does not stop me from following my interests in creative digital media. I am an energetic young man always looking for the next challenge to push the boundaries of my abilities and come up with new innovative ideas. I am proud of my skills in understanding technology and proficiency in the use of Adobe Creative Suite and Unity Game Engine. I have an interest in sketching during my free time as this enhances my creativity. I have a strong eye for design and colour co-ordination. I love working on challenging jobs in creative digital media that come my way. I see myself becoming a freelance art director or creative director.

Experience:
● Web Design & Development : HTML5, CSS, CSS3 and jQuery.
● Graphic Design & Image Editing : Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop.
● Photography : Portrait Studio, Weddings, Commercial and Art & Landscape.
● Videography : Adobe Premiere and iMovie.
● Multimedia : Adobe Animate and Unity Game Engine.
